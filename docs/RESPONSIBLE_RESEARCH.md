# Responsible Research

Agades PQC Gym is research tooling for authorized, public, toy, and downscaled settings.

## Boundaries

- Do not target live third-party systems.
- Do not generate exploit chains against real organizations or devices.
- Do not implement malware, persistence, evasion, exfiltration, or credential theft.
- Do not claim a standard is broken from estimator output alone.
- Treat estimator improvements as hypotheses until independently validated.
- Add responsible disclosure language before reporting potential implementation issues.

## Public Framing

Use sober language: evaluator-driven search over typed cryptanalytic strategies. Avoid claims that imply automatic breaks of deployed cryptography.
